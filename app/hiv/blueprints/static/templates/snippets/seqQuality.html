Sequencing quality: The graphs show the fraction of bases sequenced with a quality better than the indicated threshold for each position in read 1 and read 2 (this run used a 3x250 cycle kit). Phred qualities are estimate by CASAVA 1.8 during base calling. Things to note:
  • a slow linear decrease even for the threshold Q10. This is due to short inserts escaping size selection
  • quality deteriorates towards the end of read 2, likely due to phasing errors and optical overlap of bridge-amplified clusters on the flow cell.
  • We typically used a quality threshold of 30 for our analysis, corresponding to a miscalling rate of 0.1%.
