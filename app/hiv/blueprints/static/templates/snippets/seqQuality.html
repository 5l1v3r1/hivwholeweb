Sequencing quality: Phred quality of a typical sequencing run, estimated by CASAVA 1.8 during base calling. The slow linear decrease is due to a small fraction of sub-read-length inserts that were not successfully filtered out during size selection. The more important decrease in quality at the end of read 2 is a basic limitation of the illumina platform due to phasing errors and optical overlap of bridge-amplified clusters on the flow cell. We typically used a quality threshold of 30 for our analysis, corresponding to a miscalling rate of 0.1%.
